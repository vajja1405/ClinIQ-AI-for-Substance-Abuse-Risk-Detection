Flag Criteria 		
NC means no change in nonCC/CC/MCC designation		
1+ means the sdx is currently a NonCC and a potential candidate for bumping up		
2+ means the sdx is currently a CC and a potential candidate for bumping up a level		
2- means the sdx is currently a CC and a potential candidate for bumping down a level		
3- means the sdx is currently a MCC and a potential candidate for bumping down		
		
The criteria for the flag is as follows:		
		
"NC Criteria:        cnts1 > 200 and impact 1 <= 1.50 and level =NCC, and cnts2 <=200 and impact2<=2.25"		
                                or		
"               cnts1>200 and impact1<=1.75 and level =NCC, and cnts2>200 and impact2<=2.00"		
                                or		
               ((cnts1>200 and impact1 <= 2.25 and level =CC and cnts2>200 and impact2<= 2.75		
                                or		
                             cnts1>200 and impact1<=2.00 and level =CC and (impact2<=2.50) and (impact3<=3.00))		
                                   and		
               (cnts1>200 and impact1 >= 1.20 and level =CC and cnts2>200 and impact2>=2.1))		
                                   or		
((cnts1>200 and impact1>=1.6 and level =MCC) and ((cnts2>200 and impact2>=2.5) or (cnts3>200 and impact3>=3.1)))		
		
"1+ Criteria:      cnts1>200 and impact1 > 1.50 and level =NCC, and cnts2 >200 and impact2>2.25"		
                                or		
"             cnts1>200 and impact1>1.75 and level =NCC, and cnts2>200 and impact2>2.00"		
		
2+ Criteria:      cnts1>200 and impact1 > 2.25 and level =CC and cnts2>200 and impact2> 2.75		
                              or		
                           cnts1>200 and impact1>2.00 and level =CC and (impact2>2.50) and (impact3>3.00)		
		
2- Criteria:       cnts1>200 and impact1 < 1.20 and level =CC and cnts2>200 and impact2<2.1		
		
3- Criteria:        (cnts1<200 or (cnts1>200 and impact1<1.6 and level =MCC)) and ((cnts2>200 and impact2<2.5) 		
                                 or 		
                              (cnts3>200 and impact3<3.1))		
		
Counts 1: count of patients discharge claims with the sdx but with no other secondary diagnosis or with all other secondary diagnoses a NonCC		
Impact 1: is the “C1” impact on resource use of the secondary diagnosis for patient discharge claims in cnts1		
		
Counts 2: count of patients discharge claims with the sdx and with at least one other secondary diagnosis that is a CC but none that is a major CC		
Impact 2: is the “C2” impact on resource use of the secondary diagnosis for patient discharge claims in cnts2		
		
Counts3: count of patient discharge claims with the sdx and with at least one other secondary diagnosis that is a major CC 		
Impact 3: is the “C3” impact on resource use of the secondary diagnosis for patient discharge claims in cnts3
